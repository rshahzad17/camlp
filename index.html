About Us

Yousaf Rehmat Masih, the founder of Christ Apostolic Ministries, was a devoted servant of God whose ministry was deeply rooted in Romans 8:9 — "Now if any man have not the Spirit of Christ, he is none of His."

He began preaching the Gospel with no desire for wealth, offerings, or worldly gain, relying solely on God’s provision. A father of ten sons, he instructed each of them to serve the Lord above all else. Known for his frequent three-day fasts and unwavering faith, he was spiritually strong and held divine authority over evil spirits. His legacy continues through his disciples and followers, who now lead churches throughout Pakistan. Yousaf Rehmat Masih remained faithful to God until his final breath, a true man of faith and obedience.Before leaving this world, he entrusted all his ministerial work to his beloved son , Rev Dr Riaz Yousaf, who is now the chairman of Christ Apostlic ministries.

Over the years, our church has grown from a small home fellowship to a congregation of over 500 members. Despite our growth, we have maintained our commitment to personal relationships and community building.

We are part of the Apostolic Council of Pakistan and adhere to the doctrines of the apostolic Christian faith. Our leadership team consists of dedicated pastors, elders, and deacons who serve the congregation with love and dedication.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the Trinity: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. We believe in the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ and that salvation comes only through Him. We believe in the power of prayer and the gifts of the Holy Spirit for today's church.

Our Mission

Vision Statement

To be a beacon of God's love and truth in Lahore, transforming lives through the power of the Gospel and building a community of believers who impact their world for Christ.

Mission Statement

Our mission is threefold:

  • To worship God in spirit and truth
  • To make disciples of all nations, beginning in Lahore
  • To serve our community with compassion and practical help

Core Values

We are committed to:

  • Biblical authority in all matters of faith and practice
  • Prayer as the foundation of all ministry
  • Excellence in everything we do for God
  • Relevance to our culture while maintaining biblical integrity
  • Unity in diversity within the body of Christ
